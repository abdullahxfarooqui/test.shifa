### Cardiology
`/specialities/cardiology/islamabad`

**What does Shifa Cardiology treat?**  
Coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, heart failure, hypertension, valvular heart disease, cardiac emergencies.

**Facilities**
- Cath Labs for coronary angiography and angioplasty
- Cardiac Care Unit (CCU) with continuous monitoring
- Non-invasive cardiology suite — ECHO, ECG, stress testing
- 24/7 emergency cardiac services
- Cardiac rehabilitation and lifestyle counseling
- Coordination with cardiac surgery and ICU

**Diagnostics**
- ECG and Holter monitoring
- Echocardiography — 2D, 3D, Doppler
- Exercise stress testing and nuclear cardiac scans
- Coronary angiography and CT angiography
- Cardiac MRI
- Cardiac biomarker panels

**Treatments**
- *Emergency Cardiac Care* — heart attack, arrhythmia, cardiac arrest
- *Interventional Cardiology* — angioplasty, stent placement
- *Heart Failure & Hypertension* — medical therapy and lifestyle support
- *Arrhythmia Management* — pacemakers, defibrillators, ablation
- *Valvular Disease* — TAVI, PTMC, surgical coordination
- *Preventive Cardiology* — screening, risk modification

**Milestones**

| Period | Achievement |
|---|---|
| 1990s | Launch of cardiac services |
| 2000s | Cath Labs and CCU established |
| 2010s | Advanced interventional and non-invasive cardiology |
| 2020s | Shifa Heart Center — fully integrated with 24/7 emergency and cardiac rehab |

**When to consult:** Chest pain, shortness of breath, irregular heartbeat, fatigue on exertion, or risk factors like diabetes, hypertension, family history of heart disease.
